# Privacy Policy – Sonico Swipe

**Last updated:** March 2025

Sonico Swipe respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the Sonico Swipe mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

Sonico Swipe collects limited usage data to provide core functionality and personalize your experience. This may include:

- Songs you like or skip  
- Swipe interactions and playback actions  
- General in-app usage data  

We do **not** collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, or contacts.

---

## How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:

- Enable core app functionality  
- Personalize music recommendations based on your preferences  
- Improve app performance and user experience  

---

## Data Linking and Tracking

- The data collected is **not linked to your identity**  
- Sonico Swipe does **not** track users across apps or websites  
- We do **not** sell or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes  

---

## Data Storage and Security

Usage data is stored securely and retained only as long as necessary to operate and improve the app.

---

## Children’s Privacy

Sonico Swipe is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

---

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
